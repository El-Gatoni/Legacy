Legacy is a privacy-first, offline personal wealth tracker.

All data stays on your device — no account, no server, no telemetry.

FEATURES
• Net & gross wealth dashboard with live BTC and stock prices (optional)
• Bitcoin stack tracking with automatic P&L and DCA
• Real-estate portfolio: purchase price, current value, outstanding loans
• Savings accounts and bonds (Livrets)
• Stock portfolio with PRU tracking
• Physical and paper gold (ETC) with spot price support
• Liability tracker (loans, collateral debts)
• Wealth evolution chart with 2W / 1M / 6M / YTD / 1Y / Max filters
• Export / import JSON backup
• PIN lock
• Bilingual: French & English

PRIVACY
Legacy stores everything in the browser's localStorage on your device.
The only network requests are optional price fetches (BTC via CoinGecko,
stocks via Yahoo Finance, gold via GoldAPI.io) which you can disable.
No analytics, no ads, no tracking.
